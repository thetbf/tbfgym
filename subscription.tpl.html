Mebership Services and Subscriptions

Full Use: Our Full Use membership gives you access to the gym any time. This “unlimited” option also includes participation in all classes, as well as three free personal training sessions ($87)

Day Use: This option is perfect for the “work from home” crowd. Day Use membership hours are 6AM-6PM, Monday through Friday. All classes during these times are included, as well as three free personal training sessions ($80)

Weekend Membership: Perfect for those who use a fitness center at their work. The Weekend Membership allows access Saturday and Sunday, including all classes held those days. ($60)

Teen Memberships: This membership is available for teens 13-17 years old. They are allowed access to the gym Monday–Sunday between 2:30–5:30 p.m. Our staff will ensure that they receive an orientation to the weight room with a program designed exclusively for them. ($40)

Temporary: City Fitness provides memberships for individuals that need a gym on a temporary basis and are available for one, two or three months. Our three month temporary membership includes a free program design.

Drop-In: City Fitness allows folks to pay on a drop-in basis either daily or weekly.
